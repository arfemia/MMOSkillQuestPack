# MMOSkillQuestPack 1.0.0

First release, alongside MMO Skill Tree [[1.4.0]].

- **Emerald Wilds campaign** (11 quests): meet Ranger Wren at world spawn, raise
  your first camp, hunt your supper, run Quartermaster Bramble's supply ledger,
  push back the trorks, stand the graveyard watch, and earn the road south.
- **Howling Sands campaign** (8 quests): Dunewalker Ashkar appears where the
  desert is first discovered; quarry sandstone, fell desert hardwood, fish the
  oases, cull the Scarak hives, and face the apex predators of the dunes.
- **Branching NPC dialogue** for all three givers: progress-aware greetings, lore
  branches, a one-time bread handout, and intro options that drive the talk
  objectives.
- **Achievements**: both campaign capstones, the Campaigner of Orbis meta
  (server-first), Well Met, and zone-scoped hunter chains for both zones.
